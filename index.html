Our Story

Rooted in Community,
Grown with Heart

Rosewood Kitchen was born from a simple idea: great food starts with great relationships. In 2012, chef Mara Delvin opened our doors with a cast-iron pan, a garden out back, and an unshakeable belief that dinner should feel like family.

Today, we work with over 20 local farms within 100 miles of Maplewood — changing our menu with the seasons and letting the land lead the way. Every dish is a conversation between soil, sky, and the hands that cooked it.
